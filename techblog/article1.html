Introduction to Machine Learning

Machine learning is a field of artificial intelligence that uses statistical techniques to give computer systems the ability to "learn" from data, without being explicitly programmed.

This article will delve into the basic concepts of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning, algorithms like linear regression and decision trees, and the applications across various industries.
