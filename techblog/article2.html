Introduction to Neural Networks

Neural networks are a set of algorithms, modeled loosely after the human brain, that are designed to recognize patterns.

This article covers the fundamentals of neural networks, including perceptrons, activation functions, and deep learning architectures like convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs).
